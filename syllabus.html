Syllabus

0. Prerequisite.

1.范畴
1.1 范畴, 函子和自然变换
1.2 极限
1.3 万有性质
1.4 可表函子与Yoneda 引理
1.5 伴随函子
Chapter II. Free groups and free products.
1. Reduced normal form in free groups. Free abelian groups. Subgroups of finite index.
2. Subgroups of free groups. Schreier systems. Cayley graphs.
3. Algorithmic problems. Dehn functions. Complexity. Free products of algebras.
4. Free products of groups. Ping-Pong lemma. Representations of free groups by 2x2 matrices. Residually finite and residually-p groups.<
Chapter III. Wreath products.
1. Examples. Kalouzhnine-Krasner theorem.
Chapter IV. Burnside's problems.
1. Burnside-Schur theorem on linear groups.
2. Golod-Shafarevich inequality. Golod's examples.
3. Grigorchuk groups.
Chapter V. Tensor products.
1. Tensor product of modules. Hilbert's problem on piramides.
2. Tensor product of algebras. Centroids. Central simple algebras. Brauer group.
Chapter VI. Fractions.
1. Rings of fractions. Ore condition.
2. Noetherian domains. Filtrations. Examples of Noetherian domains. Division algebras.
Chapter VII. Ultraproducts.
1. Filters and ultrafilters. Limits. Ultraproducts. Malcev local theorems.
2. Lefschetz principle. Ax-Grothendieck theorem.
